## FAQ

Q: Does this cost anything?
A: No. Free, no account required, no credit card. We built it because most startups don't know they're overspending until someone shows them the number.

Q: How do you know the pricing is accurate?
A: Every number in the audit engine traces back to an official vendor pricing page, verified the week of submission. The source URLs are in PRICING_DATA.md. We check for updates weekly.

Q: Why do you ask for my email?
A: We don't — until after you see your results. Email capture comes after the audit, never before. If your savings are significant, we'll let you know about Credex credits. If you're already spending well, we'll notify you when something changes that affects your stack.

Q: What is Credex?
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits from companies that overforecast their usage. The discount is real — typically 20-35% off retail. StackAudit is the free tool we built to help founders figure out if they're a fit for those credits.

Q: Can I share my results?
A: Yes. Every audit gets a unique URL you can share. Identifying info like your email is stripped from the public version — only the tools and savings numbers show.
