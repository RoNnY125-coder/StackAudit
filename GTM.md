# GTM

## Who This Is Actually For

The person I'm building this for is a CTO at a 10-20 person startup. Not a 500-person company, not a solo dev. Specifically the kind of person who gets 6 different AI tool invoices every month, pays them all without looking too hard, and has a vague feeling they're wasting money but never sits down to figure out where.

They're usually 2-3 years into building something, have a small engineering team, and are starting to feel pressure on burn rate. AI tools crept into the stack one by one — someone added Cursor, someone else was already paying for Copilot, the company has a ChatGPT Team subscription, and now there's overlap nobody noticed.

The specific job title: CTO, Head of Engineering, or Founding Engineer at a B2B SaaS company between seed and Series A. Monthly AI spend is probably $400-2000 across the team. They have never compared these tools against each other side by side.
